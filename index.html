The Artist

Katheryn Elizabeth Hudson, known professionally as Katy Perry, is an American singer-songwriter and television judge. She began her music career singing gospel music as a teenager but is most known for her pop songs, becoming a staple in the mainstream music industry. Perry had setbacks when beginning her pop stardom when two record lables dropped her. However, Capitol Records believed in Perry. She produced the album, One of the Boys (2008). Its fun, second single, “Hot N Cold,” became viral, sold more than one million copies in the United States and placed third on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100. Perry's later produced albums went from dreamy candy-pop, Teenage Dream (2010) — to a more empowering album, Prism (2013) — to a more dance-mix genre with Witness (2017), produced after a four-year-long break and a whole new look.

Perry's pop-rock music style contains catchy beats, and relatable lyrics I enjoy. Often playing on the radio, her music captures the attention of all ages to tune into her songs. One listen, and her songs will be replaying in your head.

For more information on Katy Perry's music and tour dates, visit her official website.
